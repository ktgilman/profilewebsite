About Me

For the past 8 years, I have worked in the field of finance, but at night, I have been programming websites. Through that process, I realized that coding those websites was what I was ultimately most passionate about. As I taught myself through trial and error, I found that there was nothing more satisfying than getting a complicated piece of code to work just like I wanted it to. My goal is to become a successful developer helping to create the applications of tomorrow.

My Family

Nothing is more important to me than my family. My son Grant was born in 2014 and he is my life. Although I enjoy coding, there is nothing like coming home to his smiling face!

My wife Kathleen and I also have two amazing dogs, Lucy and Gus. We definitely have a busy household!

My Hobbies and Causes

In addition to coding, I am also a musician. I play trumpet, guitar, piano, and drums. I also sing tenor and have performed with many choirs including The Louisville Chorus, Christ Church Cathedral Choir, and St Francis of Rome Holiday Schola.

I have been associated with Menswork Inc. for about 4 years now. Our mission is to eliminate all forms of violence against women. It has been rewarding watching a startup nonprofit grow and affect change.

In addition to Menswork, I can typically be found at various fundraisers around town and enjoy freezing every February when I take the Polar Bear Plunge into the Ohio River to benefit Special Olympics.

Work Preferences

I consider myself very flexible when it comes to how I work and what I work on. I enjoy being challenged, learning on the fly, and creating solutions. Working well in a group or on my own, I can effectively get the job done in a number of different ways. Goal-oriented, I prefer to set manageable benchmarks for myself and then do everything to achieve them.
